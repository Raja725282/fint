Frequently Asked Questions
How long does the onboarding process take?

The complete onboarding process typically takes 2-3 weeks, depending on your pace and the complexity of your setup.

What support is available during onboarding?

You have access to our dedicated onboarding team, live chat support, and your assigned mentor throughout the process.

Can I complete steps out of order?

While we recommend following the sequence, you can skip ahead if needed. Some steps may depend on others being completed first.

What happens after I complete onboarding?

You'll have full access to all Flint systems and ongoing support. Your mentor will continue to be available for guidance.
